Welcome to iTrust Cares

Because every stage of life deserves care, hope, and respect

Moving a loved one into senior living is a major life change for both residents and families. New routines, new faces, and unfamiliar surroundings can bring feelings of anxiety, depression, confusion, or loneliness.

We believe in preventing issues before they arise. Our proactive approach includes regular check-ins, preventive screenings, and wellness programs designed to maintain and improve cognitive health.

Our Approach to Care

Emotional support meets clinical expertise

Two distinct but connected approaches ensure each individual receives both compassionate attention and professional care, promoting mental wellness, dignity, trust, and lasting quality of life.

Proactive Care

We believe in preventing issues before they arise, with regular check-ins, preventative screenings, and wellness programs designed to maintain and improve cognitive health.

  • Regular cognitive assessments
  • Preventative mental health strategies
  • Wellness education and resources
  • Early intervention programs

Acute Care

When immediate attention is needed, our team provides responsive, compassionate care to address acute mental health and cognitive concerns with dignity and expertise.

  • Crisis intervention
  • Specialized treatment plans
  • Family support and education
  • Coordination with other healthcare providers
