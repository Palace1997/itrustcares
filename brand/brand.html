Messaging
Mental health care for senior living.

iTrust Cares provides on-site psychiatric and cognitive support for older adults in senior living communities across South Carolina's Upstate and Lowcountry. Our nurse practitioners and physician assistants deliver unrushed, person-first care, right where residents live.

Care that comes to them

On-site support, no travel, no disruption to daily life.

Unrushed and personal

Time to listen, understand, and build real trust.

Dignity in every visit

Every resident is treated as a whole, valued person.

Logo

Using the mark

Primary, on light backgrounds
Reverse, on deep green

Do

  • Keep clear space around the mark
  • Use green on light, white on dark
  • Scale proportionally

    • Don't

  • Recolor, stretch, or rotate it
  • Add shadows or effects
  • Place on busy photos without a scrim
    • Logo Usage09
    Iconography

    A friendly, consistent icon set

    Style: simple line icons, 1.8 stroke, rounded caps and joins, one color (sage or white on dark, deep green on light). Keep them friendly and uncluttered. Avoid filled, heavily detailed, or novelty icons.

    Layout

    Spacing and balance

    Let it breathe

    Generous margins and whitespace keep the brand calm and premium. Never crowd the edges.

    Center the story

    Balance content toward the middle of the space, not clustered at the top.

    One idea per section

    Give each message room. Clear hierarchy over dense pages.

    Consistent rhythm

    Reuse the same spacing steps so pages feel like one family.

    Imagery

    How we show care

    Use: real, warm moments of older adults with caregivers and clinicians, natural light, genuine expressions, on-site settings.

    Avoid: masks, staged or clinical stock, and gloomy tones. Add a soft deep-green scrim when text sits over a photo.
