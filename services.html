Our Services

Care that comes to your community

Professional mental health and cognitive support delivered on-site, so older adults can get the care they need without ever leaving home.

On-site Care

On-site mental health care allows older adults to receive professional support without needing to leave their community. With iTrust Cares, seniors can access services tailored to their unique needs, helping them manage conditions like depression, anxiety, or memory concerns while continuing to preserve their quality of life.

Screenings

Our screenings for older adults are designed to identify early signs of depression, anxiety, cognitive decline, and other mental health challenges, often before symptoms become overwhelming. Using personalized diagnostic assessments and evidence-based tools, the iTrust Cares staff create a clear picture of each patient's emotional and cognitive wellness, so we can recommend targeted, compassionate care plans.
