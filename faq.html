Frequently Asked Questions

Helpful Answers for Patients

Get the answers you need, and stay informed and confident at every step.

At our practice, we believe quality mental health care should be accessible and affordable. We accept an extensive array of insurance plans, including but not limited to SC Medicare, BCBS, Humana, Aetna, United Healthcare, and several commercial insurance plans.

Don't see your insurance listed? Give us a call, and we'll be happy to check your coverage.
Upstate: 864-970-2273  |  Lowcountry: 843-285-6757

We prioritize timely access to care, with new patient appointments typically available within 2 to 3 weeks. We conscientiously leave spaces in our calendar to accommodate initial consultations, symbolizing our responsiveness and commitment to new clients.

If you are experiencing side effects from medication or need to relay a message in between appointments, you can reach out to our office in the Upstate at 864-970-2273 or Lowcountry at 843-285-6757 via text or call, and one of our support staff members will be able to assist you.

You can also email clinical@itrustcares.com.

There is no need to travel to us. Our providers deliver care on-site at the senior living communities we partner with across the Upstate and Lowcountry, meeting each resident in the familiar, comfortable setting of their own community.

If your community is not yet a partner, reach out and we will be glad to talk with your team about bringing our services on-site.

Our nurse practitioners and physician assistants specialize in the emotional and cognitive well-being of older adults. We support residents navigating depression, anxiety, grief and loss, adjustment to community living, and cognitive changes such as those related to dementia.

Several of our providers are Certified Dementia Practitioners, and every visit is unrushed and centered on the person, not just a diagnosis.
