Partners

Caring for the whole person

True wellness comes from treating both the mind and body, so we collaborate with trusted partners to surround our patients with the support they deserve.

To further support our patients' emotional well-being, iTrust Cares proudly partners with SoCalm SC, a trusted mental health organization. When a patient needs additional counseling or therapeutic care, we collaborate seamlessly with SoCalm SC to connect them with qualified mental health professionals. Together, we provide a care approach that supports both the mind and body, because true wellness comes from treating the whole person.
