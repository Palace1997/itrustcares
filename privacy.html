Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters

This policy explains how iTrust Cares handles information submitted through this website.

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

iTrust Cares (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information you provide when you visit itrustcares.com or contact us through this website. It does not describe our handling of protected health information (PHI) in a clinical setting, which is governed by our separate Notice of Privacy Practices.

Information we collect

When you use our contact or partnership forms, we may collect the name, email address, organization, and message details you choose to provide. We ask that you do not submit personal medical or health information through website forms. We may also collect standard technical information such as browser type and pages visited to help us improve the site.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your questions and requests
  • To coordinate potential partnerships with senior living communities
  • To improve the content and performance of our website

How we protect your information

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information you share with us. Please remember that no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we encourage you to call our office for any patient-related or sensitive matters.

Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our website and communications, and as required by law.

Your choices

You may contact us at any time to ask what information we hold about you or to request that we update or delete it.

Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email clinical@itrustcares.com or call the Upstate at 864-970-2273 or the Lowcountry at 843-285-6757.
