Our Providers

Meet the people behind the care

Lindsey Smith

MSN, APRN, FNP-C, CDP

Originally from Rock Hill, South Carolina, Lindsey earned a BA in Spanish from NC State, a BSN from Queens University, and an MSN from Clemson University, and is a Certified Dementia Practitioner. After beginning her career in home health with the geriatric population in assisted living, she developed a deep passion for one of our most vulnerable groups. She takes a collaborative approach, working closely with patients, families, and facility staff to build care plans that serve everyone involved. Outside work she enjoys time with family and friends, the beach, and walking her dogs, Bob Barker and Sadie.

Caroline West

MSN, APRN, AGNP-C, CDP

Originally from Savannah, Georgia, Caroline earned her nursing degree from the University of South Carolina and completed her MSN at South University in 2003 with a focus in Adult-Gerontology. She has a deep appreciation for each patient's life story and a strong awareness of how often mental health concerns in the elderly are overlooked or mislabeled. Caroline believes warm conversation and active listening are essential to effective care. In her free time she enjoys movies, reading, thrifting, traveling, and exploring new restaurants.

Hannah David

MSPAS, PA-C, CDP

Born and raised in Northeast Georgia, Hannah completed her BS in Interdisciplinary Studies with a focus in Biomedical Ethics at Young Harris College before earning her MSPAS from Brenau University. She holds certifications in Basic Life Support, MOCA, Clozaril REMS, and Medication-Assisted Treatment. Passionate about caring for the geriatric population, she values iTrust Cares' specialized, high-touch approach, including in-person follow-up visits that provide deeper insight into mental health concerns. In her free time she enjoys reading, time with family, and traveling.

Renee Bridges

MSN, APRN, FNP-C, CDP

Originally from a small town in Illinois, Renee moved to the Upstate of South Carolina at age six and has lived here most of her life. Nursing is her second career: she earned her BSN from USC Upstate in 2009 and her MSN from Walden University in 2018. Since becoming a nurse practitioner she has devoted her career to the geriatric population, believing small interventions can greatly enhance quality of life. She cares for each person as she would a loved one, prioritizing meaningful conversation and trust. Outside work she enjoys family, her puppy, reading, and crafts.

Melissa “Misty” Danner

MSN, APRN, AGNP-BC, CDP

Born in Sumter, SC, on Shaw Air Force Base, Misty has lived in the Upstate for more than 20 years. She earned her undergraduate degree in Biology from USC Upstate and completed both her nursing and Master's degrees at Clemson University, with certifications in Advanced Life Support and as a Dementia Specialist. Misty has always had a deep passion for senior mental health, believing aging adults should be treasured, supported, and reminded they are valued. In her free time she loves the outdoors, hiking, biking, gardening, and swimming.

Lily Rigmaiden

PA

Originally from Denver, Colorado, Lily grew up hiking, camping, and spending time outdoors. She earned her Bachelor's in Criminal Justice and Psychology from the University of Northern Colorado and her Master of Physician Assistant Studies from Drexel University, and is certified through the NEI Master Psychopharmacology Program. She has a special passion for the geriatric population and is committed to safe, effective, collaborative care alongside families and support teams. Lily moved to the Upstate with her husband and son in June 2024, and enjoys time with her family, dog, and cat, plus reading, kayaking, camping, and traveling.
